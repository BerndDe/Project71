School Life

TPS has a warm environment making school an enjoyable experience for all 200 kids, carrying out it's core values of 'Calm, Courteous, Considerate and Calm. There are many fun lessons to do at school inluding English, Maths, Science and More. Across the whole school there are two groups of childern, Pre-Prep and Prep with Receptcion, Year 1 and 2 in Pre-Prep, and Years 3-8 in Prep. There are also maning exciting clubs to take part in, includign Crricket Club, Origami Club, Drumming Club and Rocket Club to name a few.

Kempton Cricket Ground

For all of our home sports fixtures of rugby, football and cricket, they are played here at Kempton Cricket Ground, home of TPS Sports. We also hold our cross country event here.

Nature Trail

Outdoor learning is an important part of the EYFS curriculum at Twickenham Prep where our Nature Trail is used to extend and enhance the outdoor learning provision. The children have the freedom to explore and experience the outdoors using multiple senses. In discovering where creatures live, making potions, creating stories, building dens and using simple tools, the children learn to respect the environment and grow in confidence. Weekly sessions are delivered by our Forest School Leader following the Forest School principles of child-led learning that use the outdoors as an inspirational, educational environment.
